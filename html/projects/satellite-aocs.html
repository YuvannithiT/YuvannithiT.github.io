Experience Breakdown

During my project on the modeling and simulation of a satellite Attitude and Orbit Control System (AOCS) with reaction wheels, I was responsible for developing a comprehensive closed-loop control model using Simulink. The project focused on designing a system to achieve accurate satellite reorientation and angular rate control. Below are the key highlights of my contributions and achievements:

  • Developed a closed-loop control model featuring a pyramidal configuration of reaction wheels, ensuring precise attitude control and safety in satellite design.
  • Formulated and analyzed the satellite’s moment equations and the dynamics of the reaction wheels to predict the satellite’s behavior in response to control inputs.
  • Implemented quaternion kinematics for state propagation and a Proportional-Derivative (PD) control strategy using quaternion error matrices, chosen for their stability and precision.
  • Integrated the PD control and quaternion kinematics into the Simulink model, achieving reliable control over satellite reorientation and angular rates.

To overcome technical challenges, I undertook the following:

  • Computed the torque distribution matrix for the pyramidal reaction wheel configuration, ensuring accurate application of control torques.
  • Developed specific transformations to manage the physical layout of reaction wheels and the mathematical principles of torque distribution.

Testing and validation efforts included:

  • Created a comprehensive Software-in-the-Loop (SIL) test plan addressing challenges such as hardware-in-the-loop integration and noise modeling.
  • Verified and validated the control system’s performance to ensure readiness for actual deployment.

Through this project, I demonstrated:

  • Technical proficiency in Simulink and control systems modeling.
  • Analytical skills in deriving and solving dynamic equations for satellite motion and reaction wheel dynamics.
  • Problem-solving abilities in addressing torque distribution and noise modeling challenges.
  • The capacity to manage and deliver solutions to complex open-ended design challenges within time constraints.

This experience honed my technical and analytical skills while showcasing my ability to deliver high-quality results in dynamic and challenging scenarios.
