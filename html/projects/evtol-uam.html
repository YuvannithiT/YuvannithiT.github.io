Experience Breakdown

Over the course of four months, our three-member team successfully designed and implemented a novel autopilot control system for a Vertical Takeoff and Landing (VTOL) Urban Air Mobility (UAM) vehicle. The primary goal was to ensure stability and performance during takeoff, waypoint navigation, and landing, addressing challenges posed by the hybrid electric propulsion system. Below are the key contributions and outcomes:

  • Designed a closed-loop control system and created a high-fidelity simulation environment for testing.
  • Collaborated on a PID control system tailored to the aircraft's eight variable pitch rotors and distributed electric drive.
  • Developed a comprehensive simulation model using MATLAB and Simulink to test autopilot control algorithms.
  • Ensured stability and maneuverability throughout the flight envelope, adapting to varying conditions and wind disturbances.
  • Facilitated testing and refining of autopilot control algorithms for optimal performance.

Additional contributions included:

  • Conducting trade studies on critical metrics such as aircraft endurance and hybrid electric propulsion system integration, guiding design decisions.
  • Establishing a real-time connection to the diesel-electric hybrid engine for power generation testing and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) simulation.
  • Collaborating with propulsion system engineers and technicians using industry-standard microcontrollers, CAN bus, and FlightGear software.

The project also involved:

  • Simulating flight profiles, performance, and propulsion/throttle control with a two-way communication portal connected to a physical diesel-electric hybrid engine.
  • Visualizing the generated power and flight dynamics using FlightGear software.

This experience deepened my understanding of flight control systems and hybrid propulsion integration while enhancing my skills in tackling complex engineering challenges. It also provided valuable insights into testing, simulation, and real-time system interactions in aerospace applications.
