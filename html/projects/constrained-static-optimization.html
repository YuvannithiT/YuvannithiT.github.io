Experience Breakdown

Over a four-month period, I spearheaded a project focused on maximizing an airliner's performance within strict operational constraints. The objective was to optimize the aircraft's climb performance while adhering to specific flight envelope limitations using advanced optimization techniques.

Below are the key contributions and highlights of the project:

  • Developed a comprehensive 6-DOF nonlinear flight dynamics model in Simulink to capture essential aircraft dynamics across all axes and movements.
  • Calculated trim settings to maintain steady, level flight at varying speeds and altitudes.
  • Employed the steepest descent gradient algorithm to enhance the airliner's climb rate within designated mission and operational parameters.
  • Performed simulation iterations to rigorously evaluate equilibrium flight dynamics stability and maximize climb rate across various trim values.
  • Validated the optimization process by rerunning it using MATLAB's fmincon function, achieving 98% convergence to expected climb rates.
  • Extensive simulations confirmed the optimized climb profile, ensuring stable equilibrium with the desired maximum climb rate.

This project provided valuable experience and skill development in the following areas:

  • Advanced optimization techniques, particularly in the context of aerospace engineering.
  • Aircraft modeling, flight dynamics, and control theory.
  • Practical applications of optimization algorithms and simulation analysis in aviation.

Overall, the project reinforced my technical expertise in optimizing airliner performance and demonstrated the effectiveness of simulation and algorithmic approaches in achieving aerospace objectives.
